Meet Antoine

I'm a freelance ML Researcher & Engineer based between Paris and Marseille, with 9+ years of experience designing end-to-end machine learning systems.

My journey

After graduating from ISAE Supaero with a dual major in Machine Learning and Statistics, I spent several years sharpening my skills across startups and large tech companies.

  • - At Neko Health (cofounded by Hjalmar Nilsonne & Daniel Ek), I led ML for dermatological diagnostics — from lesion detection to representation learning and longitudinal tracking, helping build a next-gen preventive health platform.
  • - As a freelance engineer, I delivered CV, NLP, and predictive modeling solutions for clients like Cyclope.ai (Vinci Group), BCG Gamma, Natixis, Webedia, and Accenture.
  • - I also worked at Criteo, where I developed statistical models and analytics tools to optimize performance at scale across international ad campaigns.

I'm passionate about computer vision, health tech, and AI systems that create real-world impact. I thrive in fast-paced, mission-driven teams where ideas can go from concept to production quickly. Let's connect and make something great together.

Outside of work, I practice yoga and climbing, and enjoy building mindful, balanced routines.
