Full-time Freelancer

Red e App was growing like crazy when I was hired to start helping with design…

The first thing we tackled was the logo. Red e App needed a refreshed logo that would reflect sophistication & future aspirations and look good in a high variety of situations. The “e” mark was required to remain in near form to its existing state.

The process was highly collaborative and involved multiple iterations & feedback cycles. The logo has been well received by Red e App’s staff and their clients and has surprised even me with its versatility.

Next was a whole lot of things: I worked on the mobile app (their main product), the corresponding web app and admin interface, presentation slides and other marketing materials. Then we got to the website. A few of these screenshots are shown below.
