Full-time Freelancer

Full-time Freelancer is a short book that covers my revenue and expenses as a full-time freelance designer in 2010 & 2011.

It’s a guide for those who are thinking about going out on their own but are unsure of how much money you need to make to support yourself (and a family), what paying your own taxes looks like, how to pay yourself a salary, and other aspects related to running your own business.

Many years ago I was a burgeoning web designer with my sights set on freelancing full-time. However, I had no idea how much money I needed to make to match the equivalent of my full-time job. I knew it was more, but how much more? Other unknowns plagued me as well: like how much was I going to need to save for taxes, or how did I even pay taxes?

I went to a “how to start your own business” seminar and read a lot of blog posts but confusion still reigned. All I really wanted to see was how much other people made and spent — I just wanted a reference point.

Now I’m my own reference point and so I put it in a book in case it might help others.
